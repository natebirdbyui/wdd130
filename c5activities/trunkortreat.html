The times are changing. The days are getting shorter. We're feeling the crisp, cool air in the morning. The days are getting spookier! It's time for the ward Trunk-or-treat!

This year we have been invited to a Tri-Trunk-or-Treat will be at the Montana Building on Saturday, October 26, @ 6pm.

Yes, you heard that right! It will be a Three-Ward Trunk or Treat! We will be having cookies and hot cider.

If you are participating in the trunk-or-treat by passing out candy. Please park your trunk adjacent to the sidewalk around the church. On the map you can see color markings. Orange is for trunk or treat cars for passing candy. Purple is for potential overflow for handing out candy. Green area on the map is for parking.

If you are participating by decorating your spooky car, please arrive at 5:00pm to setup.
